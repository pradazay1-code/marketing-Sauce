# Welcome Email Template

## New Client Welcome Email

```
Subject: Welcome to [Agency Name]! Here's What's Next 🎉

Hey [Client First Name],

We're pumped to officially welcome [Business Name] to the [Agency Name] family!

Here's what happens next — we keep this simple:

**Step 1: Onboarding Questionnaire (5 min)**
We'll send you a quick questionnaire to make sure we understand your business, goals, and brand inside and out.

**Step 2: Kickoff Call**
We'll schedule a 30-minute kickoff call to align on strategy, priorities, and the game plan for month one.

**Step 3: We Get to Work**
After kickoff, we hit the ground running. You'll see your first deliverable within [X days].

**What we need from you:**
- [ ] Complete the onboarding questionnaire (link coming shortly)
- [ ] Send over brand assets (logo, colors, fonts) if you have them
- [ ] Grant us access to [relevant accounts — Google Analytics, social media, etc.]

**Your main point of contact:** [Your Name] — [email] / [phone]

Don't hesitate to reach out anytime. We're here to make [Business Name] shine.

Let's get it!

[Your Name]
[Agency Name]
```

## Onboarding Questionnaire Follow-Up

```
Subject: Quick questionnaire for [Business Name] — takes 5 minutes

Hey [Client First Name],

Here's the onboarding questionnaire we mentioned. This helps us build your strategy:

1. Describe your business in one sentence.
2. Who is your ideal customer? (Age, location, interests)
3. Top 3 business goals for the next 6 months?
4. What marketing have you tried before? What worked?
5. Who are your top 3 competitors?
6. What makes you different from them?
7. Do you have brand guidelines? (Colors, fonts, logo)
8. Monthly marketing budget?
9. How do you prefer to communicate? (Email, phone, text)
10. Anything else we should know?

Just reply to this email with your answers — doesn't need to be perfect. We'll discuss everything on our kickoff call.

Talk soon!

[Your Name]
```
