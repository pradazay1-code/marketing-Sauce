# Outreach Email Templates

## Template 1: The Observer
*Best for: Businesses with obvious marketing gaps*
```
Subject: Quick thought on [Business Name]

Hey [First Name],

I was looking at [Business Name] online and noticed [specific observation — e.g., "your website doesn't show up when I search '[their service] in [their city]'"].

That's actually a quick fix — and it could bring you a lot more [customers/clients/bookings].

We just helped a [similar industry] business [specific result — e.g., "go from page 3 to the top of Google in 6 weeks"]. Happy to share what we did if you're curious.

Worth a quick chat?

[Your Name]
```

## Template 2: The Complimenter
*Best for: Businesses doing some things well but missing opportunities*
```
Subject: Love what [Business Name] is doing

Hey [First Name],

Been following [Business Name] — really like [specific compliment — e.g., "the branding on your Instagram" or "your customer reviews"]. You're clearly doing something right.

One thing I noticed that could amplify what you're already doing: [specific opportunity — e.g., "running some targeted ads to your best posts could easily 3-4x your reach"].

Would you be open to a 10-minute call? I'll share exactly what I'd do — no pressure at all.

[Your Name]
```

## Template 3: The Case Study
*Best for: Leads in industries where you have results to show*
```
Subject: How a [industry] business got [specific result]

Hey [First Name],

We recently worked with a [industry] business similar to [Business Name] and helped them [specific result with numbers — e.g., "generate 47 new leads in 30 days through Google Ads"].

I looked at [Business Name] and honestly think you could see similar (or better) results — especially with [specific opportunity you noticed].

Want me to send over a quick breakdown of what we'd do?

[Your Name]
```

## Template 4: The Free Value
*Best for: Building goodwill before asking for anything*
```
Subject: 3 quick wins for [Business Name]

Hey [First Name],

I was looking at [Business Name]'s online presence and put together 3 quick things you could do right now to [get more customers/improve your marketing/etc.]:

1. [Specific, actionable tip]
2. [Specific, actionable tip]
3. [Specific, actionable tip]

No strings attached — just thought these might help. If you want to chat about implementing any of them, I'm happy to help.

[Your Name]
```

## Template 5: The Referral/Mutual Connection
*Best for: When you have a mutual connection or referral*
```
Subject: [Mutual connection] suggested I reach out

Hey [First Name],

[Mutual connection name] mentioned you might be looking for help with [service]. We work with [industry] businesses on exactly that.

Recently helped [similar business] [specific result]. Would love to see if we could do something similar for [Business Name].

Open to a quick call this week?

[Your Name]
```

## Follow-Up Templates

### Follow-Up 1 (Day 3)
```
Subject: Re: [Original subject]

Hey [First Name],

Just bumping this up — know you're busy. Wanted to see if [specific value prop] would be helpful for [Business Name].

Happy to work around your schedule.

[Your Name]
```

### Follow-Up 2 (Day 7)
```
Subject: Quick idea for [Business Name]

Hey [First Name],

Different angle — I was thinking about [Business Name] and [new observation or free tip related to their business].

[1-2 sentences expanding on the idea]

Figured you'd want to know. Let me know if you ever want to chat about this.

[Your Name]
```

### Follow-Up 3 — Break Up (Day 14)
```
Subject: Should I close your file?

Hey [First Name],

I've reached out a couple times about helping [Business Name] with [service]. Totally understand if the timing isn't right — no hard feelings at all.

If things change down the road, my door's always open. Wishing you and [Business Name] the best!

[Your Name]
```
