About the Studio

North Atlantic Tattoo is a custom tattoo studio located in the heart of historic New Bedford, Massachusetts. Founded on a commitment to artistry and craftsmanship, our studio is a space where timeless design meets contemporary tattoo culture.

With a combined 45 years of tattooing experience, our artists specialize in creating one-of-a-kind pieces tailored to each client. Every tattoo begins with a conversation — we take the time to understand your vision and bring it to life with precision and artistry.
