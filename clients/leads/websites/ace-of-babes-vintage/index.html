Welcome to Ace of Babes Vintage

Ace of Babes Vintage is a proud vintage clothing shop serving the Worcester, MA community. Every piece in our shop has a story. We curate the best vintage finds so you can express your unique style.

We believe in quality, community, and creating an experience that keeps you coming back. Whether you're a first-time visitor or a longtime regular, you're always welcome here.
