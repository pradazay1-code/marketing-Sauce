About Us

Restaurant in Somerset, MA

Slades Ferry Grille honors Somerset's rich history while bringing a fresh, modern dining experience to the iconic space that once housed Magoni's Ferry Landing for over 70 years. After extensive renovations, we're proud to carry on the tradition of great food and warm hospitality on the waterfront.
