About Us

Mexican Restaurant & Bar in Fall River, MA

Morgan's Cantina is a waterfront Mexican restaurant on the Fall River waterfront, offering stunning views of Mount Hope Bay. Owners Michael and Nicole Lund bring handmade authentic Mexican cuisine and fresh craft cocktails to a beautifully renovated waterfront space. From tacos and enchiladas to margaritas and tequila flights — it's all here.
