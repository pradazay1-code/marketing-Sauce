# Business Goals

## Vision
Build a highly efficient, AI-powered marketing agency that delivers exceptional results for clients while maximizing automation and minimizing manual overhead.

## Short-Term Goals (Next 30 Days)
- [ ] Set up the full system and test all workflows
- [ ] Find first 50 leads
- [ ] Send outreach to top 20 leads
- [ ] Book 5 discovery calls
- [ ] Close first client
- [ ] Build first client website or deliverable

## Medium-Term Goals (Next 90 Days)
- [ ] Have 3-5 active paying clients
- [ ] Establish consistent lead generation pipeline (50+ leads/month)
- [ ] Build a portfolio of 3+ websites
- [ ] Run ad campaigns for at least 2 clients
- [ ] Hit $5,000/month recurring revenue
- [ ] Refine all SOPs based on real experience

## Long-Term Goals (6-12 Months)
- [ ] 10+ active clients
- [ ] $15,000-25,000/month recurring revenue
- [ ] Hire first team member or VA
- [ ] Build agency website and brand presence
- [ ] Develop case studies from client results
- [ ] Automate 80%+ of backend operations

## Revenue Targets
| Milestone | Monthly Revenue | Clients Needed |
|---|---|---|
| Foundation | $2,000-5,000 | 2-3 |
| Growth | $5,000-15,000 | 5-8 |
| Scale | $15,000-30,000 | 10-15 |
| Premium | $30,000+ | 15+ or higher-ticket clients |

## Notes
- Update these goals monthly
- Celebrate wins — track milestones hit
- Adjust based on what's actually happening in the business
