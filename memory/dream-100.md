# Dream 100

## What is the Dream 100?
The top 100 businesses, brands, or individuals you'd love to work with. These are your ideal clients — the ones that would transform your agency if you landed them.

## How to Use This
1. Research and add businesses to this list
2. Nurture relationships over time (not just cold outreach)
3. Engage with their content, attend their events, add value first
4. When the time is right, reach out with a tailored pitch
5. Update status as relationships develop

## Status Options
- **Identified** — On the list, no contact yet
- **Engaging** — Commenting, sharing their content, building awareness
- **Contacted** — Sent first outreach
- **In Conversation** — Active dialogue
- **Proposal Sent** — Formal proposal delivered
- **Won** — They're a client!
- **Not Now** — Timing wasn't right, revisit later

## Notes
- Quality over quantity — better to have 50 perfect fits than 100 random ones
